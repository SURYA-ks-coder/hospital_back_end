Welcome to Thanu Hospitals

Thanu Hospitals is a multi-specialty healthcare centre which started with the motto of “Rendering Quality & Advanced Healthcare at its best” to the rural population. It started in the Month of October 2007. It’s a Fifty bedded hospital, with 24 hours critical care centre and various healthcare disciplines with centralized A/C. In our Thanu Hospitals, we believe in treating patients and rendering service in par with the international standards.
